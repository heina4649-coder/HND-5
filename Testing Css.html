Role of Project Manager

Project Manager has maximum authority in a Projectized organization and least authority in a Functional organization

In Functional organizations staff is organized based upon their specially, such as engineering or sales.

In these organizations, functional managers are responsible for specialty departments like marketing.

In Functional organization, the role of Project Manager is limited. In Projected organization, PMs have more authority and independence.

All the persons in the project team report to the Project Manager.
